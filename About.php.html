Get To Know More About Us

Tinna Medical Centre is an indigenous Zambian company which was initially established as a Drug Store in 1994. It is a subsidiary of Tinna Investments Limited, the holding company that was incorporated in 1997. TMC is registered in accordance with the provisions of Section 45 of the Health Professions Act No. 24 of 2009 of the Laws of Zambia as a Class (B) facility under the Health Professions Council of Zambia (HPCZ).

Since 1997, TMC has evolved from being a Drug Store into a Medical Centre. TMC has been permitted to trade by Kitwe City Council under the Local Government Act No. 218, Section 70 (1) and (2) of the Laws of Zambia.

The Tinna Medical Centre is a private health facility mandated to provide health services to members of the general public in order to promote a healthy and productive nation. The Centre is guided by the following legal frameworks which clearly stipulates its mandate and functions: The Health Professions Act No. 24 of 2009 of the Laws of Zambia, the Registration of Business Name Act No.16 of 2011 of the Laws of Zambia and the Local Government Act No.218, Section 70 (1) and (2) of the Laws of Zambia..

Our hospital has been serving the community for over 50 years with dedication and care. Our team of highly qualified professionals is committed to providing the best possible care for our patients.
